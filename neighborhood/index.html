Where we are

Taneytown is a quiet small town with a good deal to do close to home. There are six public parks around town, and the library is within walking distance of the community — an easy resource for families. For shopping, the Town Mall of Westminster and the Frederick Towne Mall are both a short drive away.

Getting around

Day to day, groceries, the pharmacy, and the library are all just a couple of minutes away. When residents want a bigger day out, both Baltimore, Maryland, and Gettysburg, Pennsylvania, are about an hour's drive.

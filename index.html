Life around Taneytown

Taneytown Village sits in a quiet part of Taneytown, close to the shopping, dining, and everyday places you need. Taneytown Village is in Taneytown, a small, settled city in the northern part of Carroll County near the Pennsylvania line, with an easygoing pace and both Baltimore and Gettysburg about an hour away.
